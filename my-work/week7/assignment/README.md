## Two Articles with data and data visualization

### 1 How big is the crazy rich Asian wealth gap?

https://multimedia.scmp.com/lifestyle/article/2163738/crazy-rich-asians/index.html

This article was written after the movie Crazy Rich Asians showed the reality about rich Asians not only in America but also in Asia. On the web page, you can see a series of charts and interactive visualizations about the wealth gap in Asian countries compared to the rest of the world. Also, there is an interesting interactive chart that you can exactly see how much rich asians spend on their clothes.

### 2 The dude map: How Americans refer to their bros

https://qz.com/316906/the-dude-map-how-american-men-refer-to-their-bros/

In this article, there is a visualized map (I think it is made by d3) that we can see the preferences of the word people choose to call their bros in different areas of America.
